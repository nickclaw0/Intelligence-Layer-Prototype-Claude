The positioning workshop locked the brand's strategic frame.

## Decision

[Route two, "Restoring sleep confidence," is the lead. Route three, "A clearer path through insomnia," becomes support language. Route one, "The night can change," is out]^[src:7e2a71d4]. The recommendation was stated as [Velorixa should own restored sleep confidence, expressed through calm onset, next-day relevance, and medically grounded support]^[src:7e2a71d4].

## Rationale

Route two is [the most distinctive and ownable, and it gives creative a better world to show the human cost of insomnia without melodrama]^[src:7e2a71d4]. Confidence also [helps medical review because it connects to behaviors and expectations, not a claimed clinical endpoint]^[src:7e2a71d4]. To avoid drift it is anchored in [three components: confidence to discuss insomnia, confidence to start treatment, and confidence to evaluate progress]^[src:7e2a71d4].

## Rejected alternatives

- [Route one "The night can change" was too broad, any sleep aid could say it, and it missed the next-day consequence]^[src:7e2a71d4].
- [Route three "A clearer path" was strong for HCPs but less memorable for patients, so it was kept as support language]^[src:7e2a71d4].

## Consequences

The client asked that [the proposal recommend this rather than present it as one option among many]^[src:7e2a71d4], setting up the creative and message work that follows.
