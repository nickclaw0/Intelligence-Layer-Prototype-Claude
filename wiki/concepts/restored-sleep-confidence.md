The brand's positioning platform: Velorixa owns restored sleep confidence rather than a generic promise of sleep.

## Definition

The idea grew out of the kickoff read that [the richer territory is helping people feel in control of their nights again, since the market is already crowded with functional sleep promises]^[src:623851f2]. At the positioning workshop it was chosen deliberately: [Velorixa should own restored sleep confidence, expressed through calm onset, next-day relevance, and medically grounded support]^[src:7e2a71d4]. To stop it drifting into vague empowerment, confidence is defined through three components: [confidence to discuss insomnia, confidence to start treatment, and confidence to evaluate progress]^[src:7e2a71d4]. The decks restate the spine as [a core promise of "sleep that feels possible again," supported by calm onset, a clear morning, and sustained agency]^[src:f1384ac1].

## Why it matters here

It connects to behaviors and expectations rather than a claimed clinical endpoint, which [helps medical review because confidence is tied to behavior, not a promised outcome]^[src:7e2a71d4]. The strategic reframe in the foundation deck is [to move agency to the narrative center, speak to night and next day together, and signal medical rigor without coldness]^[src:f1384ac1]. It is the through-line every later concept ladders to.
