The strategist view of how Velorixa scales from a launch brand to a durable sleep franchise.

## Definition

The growth thesis is that [sustainable growth comes from owning the routines around treatment, not just awareness of the product]^[src:0b3aa091]. After initial adoption the brand grows by [increasing appropriate identification, improving start confidence, and reinforcing persistence: find, convert, retain]^[src:0b3aa091]. A flywheel links [clinical proof, HCP routine, patient stories, support data, and cultural salience so each loop makes the next cheaper and more credible]^[src:0b3aa091]. The horizons run [year one launch trust, year two scale routines, year three franchise, year four-plus category leadership]^[src:0b3aa091].

## Why it matters here

Expansion is bounded to protect the medical center: [core insomnia first, comorbidity contexts next, wellness sleep used carefully because of trivialization risk, and sleep performance only later]^[src:0b3aa091]. The narrative platform evolves without losing its center: [now make sleep feel possible again, next make treatment easier to navigate, later make sleep confidence a category standard]^[src:0b3aa091].
