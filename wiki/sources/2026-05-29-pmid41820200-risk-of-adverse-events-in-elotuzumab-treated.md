## Summary

This is [a systematic review and meta-analysis of adverse-event risk for elotuzumab, an anti-SLAMF7 monoclonal antibody used in multiple myeloma]^[src:2eb2d0ee]. [Pooling six randomised controlled trials (N = 1,736), it found elotuzumab reduced neutropenia (RR 0.86) but increased risks of pneumonia (RR 1.30), diarrhea (RR 1.16), pyrexia (RR 1.47), and infections (RR 1.09), with elevated grade 3-4 lymphopenia, pneumonia, and cataracts]^[src:2eb2d0ee].
