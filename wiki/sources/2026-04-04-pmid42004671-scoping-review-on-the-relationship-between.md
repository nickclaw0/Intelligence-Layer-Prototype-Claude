Research source: a mechanistic scoping review. Insomnia-adjacent but biological; limited direct bearing on positioning.

## Summary

The review examined [how microRNAs relate to short sleep disorder and insomnia with short sleep duration in adults aged 18-65, analyzing seven cross-sectional studies searched through June 2025]^[src:603ddd73]. It found [specific miRNAs including miR-182-5p and miR-4433b-3p appear dysregulated in sleep disorders and may contribute to inflammatory and cardiovascular complications, while larger standardized studies are needed]^[src:603ddd73].

## Key extracts

- [Dysregulated miRNAs may play a role in sleep pathophysiology and its downstream complications]^[src:603ddd73].
- [Evidence is early; the authors call for larger, more standardized studies]^[src:603ddd73].
