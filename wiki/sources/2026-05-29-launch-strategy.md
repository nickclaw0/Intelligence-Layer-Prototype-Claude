Source page for the Launch Strategy deck, a launch model that sequences trust before reach. Confidential internal draft for the prototype.

## Summary

The launch thesis is that [the fastest launch is not the loudest, it is the one that removes hesitation first, lowering efficacy hope, safety concern, and patient shame together]^[src:d7ff25ad]. The plan is to [start narrow on high-intent moments, prove simply, then scale carefully to avoid hype that triggers concern]^[src:d7ff25ad], because [trust unlocks trial]^[src:d7ff25ad].

## Key extracts

- [Launch flywheel: evidence, education, experience, advocacy, momentum]^[src:d7ff25ad].
- [Audience priority: sleep specialists for credibility, PCPs for volume with simplification, chronic strugglers for urgency and reassurance, occasional poor sleepers lower priority]^[src:d7ff25ad].
- [Channel weighting favors HCP field and patient search over peer education and mass media]^[src:d7ff25ad].
- [One promise adapted by launch job: legitimize, reassure, equip]^[src:d7ff25ad].
- [Sequencing: proof base at T-6 to T-4, market prep at T-3 to T-1, launch months 0-3, scale months 4-12, measuring quality of adoption before volume]^[src:d7ff25ad].
