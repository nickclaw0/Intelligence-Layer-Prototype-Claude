Source page for the Brand Foundation deck, the positioning system for the brand. Confidential internal draft for the prototype.

## Summary

The deck's thesis is to [move from sedating the night to restoring agency]^[src:f1384ac1], arguing that [insomnia is a confidence problem, not a bedtime problem, and the category is clinically serious but emotionally under-branded with control as the unmet emotional asset]^[src:f1384ac1]. The brand idea is stated as [the calm return of control, owned as restored sleep confidence, not a generic promise of sleep]^[src:f1384ac1]. The strategic reframe is [make agency the narrative center, speak to night and next day together, and signal medical rigor without coldness]^[src:f1384ac1].

## Key extracts

- [Core message spine: a promise of "sleep that feels possible again," supported by calm onset, a clear morning, and sustained agency]^[src:f1384ac1].
- [Positioning whitespace is high-trust, high-agency sleep restoration, between warm comfort brands and fast-relief sedation codes]^[src:f1384ac1].
- [Experience arc: anticipate, initiate, maintain, return, repeat, each tied to a patient question]^[src:f1384ac1].
- [Brand behavior principles: never dramatize the bedroom, pair sleep with tomorrow, avoid blame language, make science legible, build ritual memory]^[src:f1384ac1].
- [Foundation workplan: define, design, validate, activate across the first 120 days]^[src:f1384ac1].
