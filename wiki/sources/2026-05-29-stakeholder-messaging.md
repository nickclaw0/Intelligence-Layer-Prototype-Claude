Source page for the Stakeholder Messaging deck, a message system for patients, HCPs, payers, and caregivers. Confidential internal draft for the prototype.

## Summary

The message logic is [one brand promise, four proof languages: stakeholders do not need different brands, they need different proof]^[src:9bb80978]. The shared promise is [helping people move from sleep uncertainty to a more reliable night and a more usable tomorrow]^[src:9bb80978], held central while [evidence, tone, and the action requested change by audience]^[src:9bb80978].

## Key extracts

- [Patient message house: "you are not failing," "the night can feel safer," "tomorrow matters," warm and free of blame]^[src:9bb80978].
- [HCP message house: identify, match, support, clinically crisp for short visits]^[src:9bb80978].
- [Payer message house: burden, appropriate use, continuation, anchored in responsible access]^[src:9bb80978].
- [Tone calibration weights clarity and empathy highest, urgency lowest]^[src:9bb80978].
- [Messaging QA rules: reduce shame, define appropriate fit, avoid overpromising, link night to day, close with one action]^[src:9bb80978].
