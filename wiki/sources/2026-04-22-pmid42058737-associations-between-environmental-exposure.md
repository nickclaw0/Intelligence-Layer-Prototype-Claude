Research source on respiratory infection risk in a large cohort. Only tangentially relevant to Velorixa: insomnia appears as one covariate, not the subject.

## Summary

The cohort study of [67,890 UK Biobank participants used geospatial greenspace data and lifestyle information in Cox regression]^[src:c4738894]. It found [a scale-dependent dual effect of greenspace, and that physical activity and not smoking reduced respiratory infection risk while male sex, older age, and insomnia increased it]^[src:c4738894]. Insomnia is a contributing risk factor here, not the focus, so this source has limited direct bearing on the Velorixa positioning beyond reinforcing that insomnia carries downstream health cost.

## Key extracts
